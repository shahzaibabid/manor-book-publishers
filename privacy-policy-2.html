Privacy Policy

When you register on our website, place an order, sign up for our newsletter, or fill out a form, we collect specific information from you.

During the registration or purchase process, you may be asked to provide details such as your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, or credit card information, as needed. However, you can also browse our website anonymously.

Why Do We Collect and Use Your Information?

The information we gather from you is used for the following purposes:

  • Personalized Experience: Your data helps us customize your experience to suit your individual preferences.
  • Improving Our Website: Feedback and data collected from users enable us to refine our website offerings and features.
  • Enhanced Customer Support: Your information allows us to efficiently respond to your requests and support inquiries.
  • Processing Transactions: Your personal information will only be used to fulfill the product or service you requested. It will never be sold, traded, or transferred to any other business without your consent, except when required to deliver the service or product.
  • Periodic Communication: The email address you provide may be used to send updates, respond to inquiries, or address other requests effectively.

Your trust and privacy are our priorities, and we are committed to safeguarding the information you share with us.

How Do We Protect Your Personal Information?

We implement various security measures to safeguard your personal details whenever you place an order, submit, or access your information.

We utilize a secure server for all transactions. Sensitive information, such as credit card details, is encrypted using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and transmitted securely to our payment gateway provider’s database. Access to this data is restricted to authorized personnel who are required to maintain its confidentiality.

Rest assured, we do not store your personal information—such as credit card numbers, social security numbers, or bank account details—on our servers after a transaction is completed.

Do We Use Cookies?

Yes, we use cookies. Cookies are small files stored on your computer’s hard drive with your consent. These files allow websites or service providers to recognize your browser and capture or retrieve specific information.

We use cookies to analyze site activity, track advertisements, and improve the overall user experience. This helps us refine our tools and offerings. Occasionally, we may partner with third-party service providers to help us better understand our visitors. These providers are only permitted to use the information collected on our behalf to enhance our business operations.

Do We Share Your Information With Third Parties?

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to unaffiliated third parties. Exceptions include trusted partners who assist in operating our website, managing our business, or providing services to you, provided they agree to keep your information confidential.

We may also disclose your information when necessary to comply with the law, enforce our website policies, or protect the safety and rights of others. Non-personally identifiable information may, however, be shared with third parties for advertising, marketing, or other purposes.

Third-Party Links

Occasionally, we may include links to third-party products or services on our website.

These third-party websites have separate and independent privacy policies. Therefore, we are not responsible for the content or activities of these linked sites. Nonetheless, we strive to maintain the integrity of our website and welcome any feedback about these external links.

Online Privacy Policy Only

This privacy policy applies solely to information collected through our website and does not extend to information collected offline.
